Theater Program

Our theater program teaches young people the art of acting and production, while promoting self-confidence, teamwork, and creativity.

Classes Offered

  • Acting Basics
  • Scene Study
  • Production Basics
  • Directing
  • Playwriting
  • Comedic Acting

The Benefits

  • Develop Self-Confidence
  • Explore Self-Expression
  • Discover Imagination
  • Learn Public Speaking Skills
  • Gain Appreciation for Storytelling
  • Explore Teamwork
  • Learn Problem Solving Skills
